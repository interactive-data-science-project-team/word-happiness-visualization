The dataset was collected by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network to produce the World Happiness Report, its annual publication that surveys the state of global happiness. The dataset includes the happiness score for each country, their socioeconomic attributes such as the GDP per capita, social support and healthy life expectancy at birth, and the survey responses such as social support and freedom to make life choices. The data covers the years from 2005 to 2017.
